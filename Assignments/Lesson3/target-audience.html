Target Audience

The target audience for "weather news happens" is primarily the elderly and the non-tech-savy. The main objective of the site is to become the homepage for people that do not know how to change their homepage and show them the weather everyday whether they want it or not.

Personas

Anna

Anna is a college student majoring in the fine arts. Anna has never used a computer until college and does not understand how they work. She purchased a new laptop and has recently found weathernewshappens.com.

Frank

Frank is a older gentleman who missed the technological revolution. He hears from a friend that you can look up information on the internet. He decides to boot up windows 95 on his off-white home PC. He searches weather and finds the weather news happens website.

Scenarios

  1. You need to check the weather for today.

  2. You need to check the weather for next week.

  3. You need to check the current air quality.

  4. You need to check the humidity today.

  5. You need to check the snow level at your nearest ski resort.
