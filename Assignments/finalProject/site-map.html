Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide an answer to the commonly asked question "How long is it?". This site acts as a database for tv shows, focusing mainly on the length of a series all together.

Target Audience

This site will cater to a demographic of all ages. But target audience will most likely be teenagers to younger adults who enjoy binge-watching many different shows.
