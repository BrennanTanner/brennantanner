Recipe Calculator Case Study

Scenarios

Scenario 1: You have found a new recipe on a website and want to save it on your app. You have a screen shot of the recipe saved on your phone.

Scenario 2: You have your recipe saved on the Recipe calculator app. Now you need to double your recipe.

Scenario 3: You have found one of your Mee-Ma's famous cookie recipes. But the handwriting is in cursive so the app scanner will not be able to pick out the recipe. You decide to add it by hand.
